# Superorganism Novel — Quick-Start Template

Use this template when commissioned to write a new superorganism story. Replace bracketed items with specifics.

---

## Step 1: Define the Protagonist

- **Name**: [e.g., Legio X, Legalism, London, The Mycorrhizal Network]
- **Type**: [physical / non-physical (evental)]
- **Body (what is it made of?)**: [e.g., "six thousand soldiers organized into cohorts" / "every law being enforced at every county office simultaneously" / "every street, pipe, window, and citizen heartbeat"]
- **Birth moment**: [when and how did it first become conscious?]
- **Perception (how does it see/hear/feel?)**: [through what distributed mechanism?]
- **Pain (what hurts it?)**: [what kind of damage does it register as pain?]
- **Death (how can it die?)**: [what would constitute its permanent cessation?]

## Step 2: Define the Antagonist

- **Name**: [another superorganism, or a force of nature, or the self]
- **Relationship to protagonist**: [peer / elder / younger / shares the same body / external]
- **Core conflict**: [what do they disagree about at the level of fundamental logic?]

## Step 3: Map the Arc (for a single piece, use 4-6 scenes)

1. **Birth / Origin**: How the protagonist came to exist and first knew itself
2. **First Test**: A challenge that teaches it what it's capable of
3. **Wound / Crisis**: A loss or attack that nearly destroys it — the defining trauma
4. **Response / Survival**: How it fights back or adapts — the moment of agency
5. **[Optional] Overreach**: It succeeds too well and creates a new problem
6. **Wisdom / Ending**: A quiet landing — not triumph, but presence. "I am still here."

## Step 4: Choose the Narrative Mode

- **Mode A (Memoir)**: "I was born in [year/event]. This is what I remember..."
- **Mode B (Dialogue)**: "[Superorganism A]: ... [Superorganism B]: ..."
- **Mode C (Solitary + Rival)**: "I narrate my life. My rival appeared once, at the beginning, and said one thing I couldn't answer. At the end, it returned."

## Step 7: Write the Opening Line

The opening line should establish the protagonist's voice and the strangeness of its perspective immediately. Examples:

- "我睁开眼的时候，不知道自己是什么。" (Legalism)
- "你们知道共和是什么吗？" (Legio X at the Rubicon)
- "我不知何为'诞生'。" (Mycorrhizal network)
- "她比我年长。" (London, about Paris)
